Graduate Student Rotations

We welcome rotation students from various UAB graduate programs including Genetics, Genomics & Bioinformatics (GGB), Cancer Biology, Immunology, and related fields.

How to Apply for Rotations

  1. Email Dr. Ma with your CV and a brief statement of interest
  2. Mention your current program and expected rotation timeline
  3. We'll schedule a meeting to discuss projects and expectations

Postdoctoral Fellowship Positions

We are actively seeking motivated postdoctoral fellows to join our team and lead independent research projects in cancer immunotherapy.

Ideal Candidates

  • Education: Ph.D. in immunology, cancer biology, computational biology, genetics, or related field
  • Experience: Background in cancer research, immunotherapy, genomics
  • Skills: Strong experimental analytical and communication skills, publication record, ability to work independently
  • Interests: Passion for translational research with clinical applications
  • Mentorship: Willingness to mentor graduate students and contribute to lab culture

Research Opportunities

  • Lead neoantigen discovery and TCR engineering projects
  • Develop novel immunotherapy combination strategies
  • Analyze large-scale genomic and immunologic datasets
  • Collaborate with clinical teams on translational studies
  • Pursue independent funding opportunities (K99/R00, individual fellowships)

Application Requirements

Please send the following materials to xma2@uab.edu:

  • Current CV including publications and conference presentations
  • Cover letter describing research interests and career goals (1-2 pages)
  • Contact information for three professional references
  • Representative publication (PDF) or thesis chapter
